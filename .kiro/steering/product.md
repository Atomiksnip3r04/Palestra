# GymBro Product Overview

GymBro is a fitness tracking Progressive Web App (PWA) with native Android support via Capacitor. The app is designed for serious athletes and bodybuilders who want to track, analyze, and improve their training.

## Core Features

- **Workout Creator**: Build custom workout routines with sets, reps, RPE, and technical notes
- **Training Diary**: Log workout sessions with detailed exercise tracking and wellness data
- **Analysis Dashboard**: Visualize training volume, intensity trends, and progress over time
- **Body Tracking**: Monitor body stats, progress photos, and muscle heatmaps
- **AI Integration**: Gemini-powered workout analysis and recommendations
- **Health Connect**: Google Fit and Terra API integration for health metrics (steps, heart rate, sleep, HRV)
- **Cloud Sync**: Firebase-backed data synchronization across devices

## Target Users

Italian-speaking fitness enthusiasts (UI is in Italian). The app prioritizes performance tracking, PR monitoring, and data-driven training insights.

## Key Differentiators

- TOON encoding format for efficient health data storage
- DOMS (Delayed Onset Muscle Soreness) tracking and insights
- Advanced metrics including 1RM estimates, volume tracking, and progression analysis
- Offline-first PWA with cloud sync capabilities
